Latest Project

Shopswell.com - April 2015 to November 2016

Since March of 2015, I've been working as design director and co-founder for a start-up company called Shopswell, loosely a community-site centered around the the online shopping experience.

Shopswell's goal is to create a service and community where users could create collections, in the form of lists, of products they recommend, are looking to purchase, or just group together. Similar to Pinterest, users could create lists based around any subject they desired, such as their photography gear or favorite kitchen tools. Unlike Pinterest, everything links back to a retail site such as Amazon, Etsy, or any other online retail store, where the items can be purchased.

Additionally, we provided features for users to ask and answer shopping related questions, write product reviews, or create and publish articles based on their list collections.

Users could also share to a feed where their followers could consume the content they created and follow other users they are interested in.
